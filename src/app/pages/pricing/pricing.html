How the fee is calculated

  1. Platform Base = order total − pass-through fees (store/processor) − taxes.
  2. Whole-order tiering locks at order creation based on the Platform Base.
  3. Tier-4 cap: when the 10% tier applies, platform fee is capped at $1,000.
  4. Milestones: the order’s total fee is allocated pro-rata across milestones.
  5. Promotions/Points apply to the platform fee (subject to limits), then rounded to minor units.

FAQ

Does the $1,000 cap apply to all tiers?

No. The cap applies only when the 10% tier (Tier 4) applies.

Are store/processor fees capped?

No. Pass-through fees (app-store or payment-processor) and taxes are separate and not subject to the platform cap.

What happens on refunds or change orders?

Refunds reverse the platform fee pro-rata. Approved change orders re-lock the tier prospectively for unpaid milestones.

What ships in MVP

Everything you need to run commissions end-to-end — nothing that slows you down.

Accounts & Profile

Sign in with email (passkey optional). Edit avatar, bio, and switch roles (Client / Creator).

Creator Verification

Submit portfolio samples & basic KYC fields to unlock storefront & payouts.

Offers & Listings

Publish commission offers. Clients can post briefs or pick predefined packages.

Orders & Milestones

Structured stages with approvals; messages are bound to each order.

Payments

Google Play at launch; auto receipts, money ledger, and withdrawals queue.

Alerts

Platform (system) & Commission (order) alerts in a clean two-channel inbox.

How it works

1) Brief ↔ Offer

Clients open a brief or choose a creator offer with clear scope & price.

2) Milestones & Fund

Agree deliverables & deadlines; deposit goes to escrow. Alerts keep both sides on track.

3) Deliver → Approve → Payout

Files, messages, approvals, and receipts live in the order. Commission tier applies automatically.
